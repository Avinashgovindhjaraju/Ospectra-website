Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free plan?
▼

The Starter plan includes up to 3 AI agents, basic workflow builder access, foundational observability features, 10,000 API calls per month, and access to our community support channels.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?
▼

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle, and we'll prorate any charges accordingly.

Is there a contract required?
▼

No contracts are required for Starter or Professional plans. Enterprise customers can customize terms based on their needs. All standard plans are month-to-month with no lock-in.

What support options are available?
▼

Starter plans include community support. Professional plans get priority email support. Enterprise customers receive 24/7 dedicated account management, technical support, and custom SLAs.

How do API calls work?
▼

API calls are counted each time your agents make requests to execute workflows, access knowledge models, or run predictions. Unused calls don't roll over to the next month. Enterprise plans include unlimited API calls.

Can you customize a plan for my organization?
▼

Absolutely! Enterprise plans are fully customizable. We can tailor features, API limits, support tiers, and SLAs to match your specific requirements. Contact our sales team for a consultation.
